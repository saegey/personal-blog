## The Good

1. Consistent lap times and no major mistakes.
2. Kept heart rate high and elevated.
3. Remounts were smooth.
4. Overall result was good and lots of racers within 30 secs ahead of me so I'm getting closer to top twenty finish.
5. Switched my gear ratio from 40x17 to 36x17 and that worked well for the profile of this course. I only really spun out on the finish line and the slight gravel road descent leading up to the climb.
6. Mud tires (Specialized Terra Pro) provided good traction in corners.

<Divider sx={{ marginY: '20px' }} />

## Needs Improvement

1. Valve cores were clogged with sealant and making it very to hard to set desired tire pressure.
2. Need more lean and better lines in tighter corners. I made some small mistakes that cost me some time.
3. Tire pressure too low and not stable enough in fast corners
4. Need to practice 30 second anaerobic power efforts for steep sections.
5. Dropouts and rear QR skewer not tight enough. I was swapping chains a few days before to a newer waxed chain and playing with tension and the rocker dropouts. Well I need to tighten up everything when I finished but I didn't. Good thing there wasn't any bad outcomes from this.
